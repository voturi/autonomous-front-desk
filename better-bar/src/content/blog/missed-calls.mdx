Missed calls rarely feel urgent in the moment, but they compound fast.
Every unanswered call is a lead that goes cold, a job that goes to a competitor,
and a customer who never tries again.

## The real cost

If even 5 percent of calls are missed, the revenue loss adds up over a month.
The impact is not only lost jobs, but also slower response times and lower reviews.
